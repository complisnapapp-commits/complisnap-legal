Privacy Policy

CompliSnap - Audit Screenshot Tool

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

TL;DR: CompliSnap collects absolutely nothing. Everything happens on your device. Your screenshots never leave your computer. We can't access your data because we never receive it. Complete privacy, by design.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 We Collect NOTHING

CompliSnap does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information, including:

  • ❌ No personal data
  • ❌ No email addresses
  • ❌ No browsing history
  • ❌ No IP addresses
  • ❌ No usage analytics
  • ❌ No cookies or tracking

2. How CompliSnap Works

2.1 Local Processing Only

All screenshot processing happens entirely in your browser:

  1. You capture or upload a screenshot
  2. CompliSnap adds a timestamp overlay locally on your device
  3. The timestamped screenshot is saved to your local browser storage
  4. When you download or export, files are saved to your local computer

At no point does any data leave your device.

3. Data Storage

3.1 Chrome Local Storage

CompliSnap uses Chrome's local storage API to save your screenshots on your device. This storage:

  • ✅ Remains on your device only
  • ✅ Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • ✅ Can be cleared by you at any time

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 No Third Parties

CompliSnap does not use, integrate with, or share data with any third-party services.

5. Permissions

CompliSnap requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To capture screenshots (only when you click the extension)
  • storage: To save screenshots locally in your browser
  • downloads: To save files to your computer
